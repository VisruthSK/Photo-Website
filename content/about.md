This is a site to house some of my photos.

I tend to shoot pedestrian things; flowers, street signs, various happenings (or non-happenings) at my home, pedestrians. Sometimes I have subjects which beget excitement or interest, but most of the time I shoot to take the photo--I enjoy the process as much as I do the result. As such, don't expect to be amazed by any photos. They're all mundane, but they attempt to be snapshots of [Ephemeral Beauty](https://www.visruth.com/writings/ephemeral-beauty/); melting memories into (digital) film, impressed upon this page so as to remember some things for ever and ever and ever long. I shoot to have fun, but I'm serving these photos to never forget.

Photos mostly shot with a Fuji XT-5 + XF 33 1.4--see EXIF. Almost all photos are SOOC because I am lazy.

Email if you want high quality files; uploads are extremely compressed (~100x smaller.)
